Guidance on the number of days lost
AR 95. The undertaking shall count the number of days lost such that the first full day and last day of absence shall be
included. Calendar days should be considered for the calculation, thus days on which the affected individual is
not scheduled for work (for example, weekends, public holidays) will count as lost days.
Disclosure Requirement S1-15 – Work-life balance
AR 96. Family-related leave include maternity leave, paternity leave, parental leave, and carers’ leave that is available
under national law or collective agreements. For the purpose of this Standard, these concepts are defined as:
(a) maternity leave (also called pregnancy leave): employment-protected leave of absence for employed women
