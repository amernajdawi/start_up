Technical screening criteria  
Substantial contribution to climate change mitigation  
The activity complies with one of the following criteria:
(a) for construction and operation of pipelines and associated infrastructure for distributing heating and cooling,
the system meets the definition of efficient district heating and cooling systems laid down in Article 2,
point 41, of Directive 2012/27/EU;
(b) for refurbishment of pipelines and associated infrastructure for distributing heating and cooling, the
investment that makes the system meet the definition of efficient district heating or cooling laid down in
Article 2, point 41, of Directive 2012/27/EU starts within a three year period as underpinned by a
contractual obligation or an equivalent in case of operators in charge of both generation and the network;
(c) the activity is the following:
(i) modification to lower temperature regimes;
(ii) advanced pilot systems (control and energy management systems, Internet of Things).
