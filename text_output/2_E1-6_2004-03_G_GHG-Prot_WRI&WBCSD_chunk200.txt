Equity share The equity share reflects economic interest, which is the extent of rights a company has to the risks
and rewards flowing from an operation. Typically, the share of economic risks and rewards in an oper-ation is aligned with the company's percentage ownership of that operation, and equity share willnormally be the same as the ownership percentage. (Chapter 3)
Estimation uncertainty Uncertainty that arises whenever GHG emissions are quantified, due to uncertainty in data inputs and
calculation methodologies used to quantify GHG emissions. (Chapter 7)
Finance lease A lease which transfers substantially all the risks and rewards of ownership to the lessee and is
accounted for as an asset on the balance sheet of the lessee. Also known as a Capital or FinancialLease. Leases other than Capital/Financial/Finance leases are Operating leases.
