Sustainability-related opportunities Uncertain environmental, social or governance events or conditions that, if they occur, could cause a potential material positive effect on the
undertaking's business model, or strategy on its capability to achieve its
goals and targets and to create value, and therefore may influence its
decisions and those of its business relationship partners with regard to
sustainability matters. Like any other opportunity , sustainability-related
opportunities are measured as a combination of an impact’s magnitude
and the probability of occurrence.
